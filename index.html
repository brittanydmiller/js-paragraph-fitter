Ding wraps your lines by inserting custom line breaks at just the right spots!

  • Vanilla JavaScript
  • No lonely final words -- Ding makes sure everyone has a buddy.
  • Overflows are logged and reported below (due to long words + short line lengths)
  • Characters are 24px wide, monospaced

(This was a code challenge -- I don't claim Ding as a useful app :)
